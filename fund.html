Important Disclaimer

Transparency is paramount at PayCoin Capital Fund. We want you to invest confidently, knowing exactly what you're getting into. Therefore, we're upfront about the inherent risks associated with our investment opportunities, many of which fall under the category of Fundraising deals.

What are Fundraising deals?

These are direct negotiations between us and private investment groups, often Venture capitalists (VCs), bypassing traditional exchanges. While this allows us to secure access to exciting, up-and-coming projects at potentially favorable terms, it also comes with some key risks:

Risk 1: Dependence on Third Parties:

As the deal finalization and sometimes even project legitimacy rely on third parties, unforeseen circumstances like the disappearance of intermediaries or project dishonesty could lead to total investment loss. PayCoin Capital Fund cannot be held responsible for such risks.

Risk 2: Increased Volatility:

OTC deals often involve less established projects than those typically found on major launchpads. While we meticulously filter based on launchpad reputation, backing investors, and other due diligence measures, these projects may still carry higher inherent volatility and risk of failure.

Risk 3: Potential Changes in Terms:

Due to the dynamic nature of the crypto market, project conditions may evolve outside our control. Investors must be prepared to accept these changes and adapt their strategies accordingly.

Our Track Record:

Despite these risks, we've maintained a strong track record thanks to our complex filters and thorough due diligence process over the past several months. We prioritize projects confirmed by top launchpads, backed by reputable investors, and thoroughly audited to mitigate potential issues.

Open Communication:

We believe in informed decision-making. Invest only if you are fully aware of the inherent risks of deals and comfortable assuming them. If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to reach out to our team.

Remember, no investment is without risk. By understanding and acknowledging these potential challenges, you can make informed choices and invest with confidence in PayCoin Capital Fund's opportunities.
